ANNOUNCEMENT


Class Suspension

Yesterday July 1, 2026, the Manila Observatory released a forecast on the developments surrounding Typoon Herkz. It is currently classed as a Super Typoon with 315 kph max 195-min winds. Its center is presently located around Surigao del Sur and is slowly moving northwestward.

No one is ever fully prepared to deal with environmental calamities; however, we would like to remind everyone to stay vigilant. Try to maintain access to reliable sources of information and watch out for any unexpected weather disturbances. We must all maintain a level of preparedness to mitigate potential risks.

The University will continue monitoring the situation and will continue to provide updates.

Stay Safe, Everyone!
